Relationships

  • Both C(A)C(A)C(A) and N(AT)N(A^T)N(AT) live in Rm\reals^mRm
    • Their dimensions add up to mmm and the subspaces are orthogonal to one another
    • That is, all vectors in C(A)C(A)C(A) are orthogonal to those in N(AT)N(A^T)N(AT)
  • Both C(AT)C(A^T)C(AT) and N(A)N(A)N(A) live in Rn\reals^nRn
    • Dimensions also add to nnn and these are orthogonal
