Null Space

The null space of a m×nm \times nm×n matrix AAA is the space N(A)N(A)N(A) of all vectors xxx such that
Ax=0Ax = 0Ax=0

Proposition:

Invertible row operations do not change the null space of a matrix AAA

Proof:

  • For GAGAGA where GGG is invertible, we have GAx=0GAx = 0GAx=0 implies Ax=G−10=0Ax = G^{-1}0 = 0Ax=G−10=0
  • Additionally Ax=0Ax = 0Ax=0 implies GAx=0GAx = 0GAx=0, so bijection
